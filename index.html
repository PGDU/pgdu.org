PGDU

PostgreSQL Down Under Incorporated, known as PGDU, is a non for profit association. Our purpose is to support the growth and education of PostgreSQL, The World's Most Advanced Open Source Database.

Membership

Are you interested in PostgreSQL growth and education? Do you support PostgreSQL and want to help? By becoming a member you will be helping to support our mission and be able to influence what we do.
